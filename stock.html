Uses:

1. This is a Stock App for helping in analyzing stocks and keeping track of your financial portfolio

2. To get the details about any stock trading in Nasdaq,AMEX,NYSE and many more, just create a watchlist with correct stock symbols.

3. You can bookmark your favourite financial Websites, so you can access from the app.

4. This App gives you real-time information on stock prices, movement, volumes, technical and fundamental analysis ect, Also provides daily, montly, 3 montsh, 6 months, 1 year and 5 year Chart for each individual Stock.

5. This is a mobile responsive app, can be used flawlessly in Desktop as well as Mobile devices

6. This App allows you virtual trading of stock and other securities, keeping track of your current stock porfolio, stock bought and sold,amount invested and profit/loss.

7. Next version of this app will include virtual stock trading game, where each user will be provided with virtual money to invest in real stocks, after a certain period(1months ect) winner would be who managed to increase their portfolio the most.

8. Also the future version will include a guide on how to manage the Portfolio, i.e, What should be the Ideal percentage of cash postion, investment, retirement saving and so on
